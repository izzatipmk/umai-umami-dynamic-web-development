ABOUT US

Origin

Umai Umami is a small independent Japanese restaurant located in Birmingham city centre which is established in January 2024. Umai Umami was born out of a passion for Japanese culture, cuisine, and hospitality. Our founder, Nureffa Khairul, envisioned a dining destination where guests could immerse themselves in the artistry of Japanese gastronomy and experience the warmth of traditional Japanese hospitality.

Goals

Our mission is simple: to delight our guests with exquisite Japanese cuisine crafted with precision, passion, and respect for tradition. We strive to create memorable dining experiences that transport our guests to the vibrant streets of Tokyo, the serene temples of Kyoto, and the bustling markets of Osaka.

Team

Led by our talented team of Japanese chefs, our kitchen is a laboratory of creativity and craftsmanship. Using the freshest seasonal ingredients sourced from local farmers and purveyors, we artfully prepare a diverse menu of sushi, bento, ramen, and traditional Japanese dishes that showcase the depth and complexity of Japanese flavors.
